                    PLUNGER LAUNCH FIX — COMPLETE ✓
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ISSUE: Ball does not shoot into playfield when plunger hits it
CAUSE: Launch velocity was too weak (6.0-15.0 m/s) vs gravity (9.8 m/s²)
FIX:   Increased velocity to 16.0-30.0 m/s + fixed touch plunger
BUILD: 853ms | ERRORS: 0

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
THE PROBLEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Old velocity formula: y = 6.0 + charge*9.0
Result: y ranges from 6.0 m/s (0% charge) to 15.0 m/s (100% charge)

With gravity 9.8 m/s² downward:

Charge 0%:   6.0 m/s → reaches height 1.84 units → y=-5.0+1.84=-3.16 ✗ FAILS
Charge 50%: 10.5 m/s → reaches height 5.62 units → y=-5.0+5.62=0.62 ✗ FAILS
Charge 100%: 15.0 m/s → reaches height 11.48 units → y=-5.0+11.48=6.48 ✓ OK

Bumpers are at y = 2.2 to 4.2

RESULT: At low charge, ball doesn't reach playfield!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
THE SOLUTION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

FIX 1: Increase Launch Velocity
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Old:  physics.ballBody.setLinvel({ x:0, y:6.0+charge*9.0 }, true);
New:  physics.ballBody.setLinvel({ x:0, y:16.0+charge*14.0 }, true);

New formula: y = 16.0 + charge*14.0 (min 16.0, max 30.0 m/s)

Result:
  Charge 0%:   16.0 m/s → reaches height 13.06 units → y=8.06 ✓ REACHES
  Charge 50%:  23.0 m/s → reaches height 26.94 units → y=21.94 ✓ REACHES
  Charge 100%: 30.0 m/s → reaches height 45.92 units → y=40.92 ✓ REACHES

FIX 2: Fix Touch Plunger (Mobile)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Old code: state.ballVel.y = 6.0 + charge*9.0;
Problem: Only updates state variable, NOT physics body (doesn't actually launch!)

New code:
  if(physics){
    physics.ballBody.setGravityScale(1.0, true);
    physics.ballBody.setTranslation({ x:2.65, y:-5.0 }, true);
    physics.ballBody.setLinvel({ x:0, y:16.0+charge*14.0 }, true);
  }

Result: Touch plunger now works with proper physics
