COORDINATE ANALYSIS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PLUNGER GEOMETRY:
  • Plunger group position: (2.65, -6.3, 0.3)
  • Knob local Y position: 0.8 (at rest) to 0.1 (pulled back)
  • Knob world Y at rest: -6.3 + 0.8 = -5.5
  • Knob height: 0.22 units
  • Knob top surface Y: -5.5 + 0.11 = -5.39

BALL CALCULATION:
  • Ball radius: 0.2 units
  • Sitting on knob top: -5.39 + 0.2 = -5.19 ≈ -5.2
  • Result: Ball centered at (2.65, -5.2, 0.3)
